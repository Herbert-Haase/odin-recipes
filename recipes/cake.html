cake

A lovely cake for the whole family to enjoy.

crafting recipe:

  • 3 Buckets of milk
  • 1 egg
  • 2 sugar
  • 3 wheat

  1. put the milk buckets in the top layer
  2. put the egg in the center
  3. put the sugar on the left and right of the middle layer
  4. put the wheat in the bottom layer
